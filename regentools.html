Serenity Prayer

God, grant me the serenity to accept the things I cannot change, courage to change the things I can, and wisdom to know the difference.

Forgiveness Truths

Forgiveness is not excusing sin.

Forgiveness is not freeing the guilty of a demand for justice.

Forgiveness is not denying your hurt or stuffing your anger.

Forgiveness is not a feeling and is not conditional.

Forgiveness is not forgetting.

Forgiveness is not trust.

Forgiveness is not reconciliation.

You don't need to forgive God.

You can't "forgive yourself."

Forgiveness Prayer

Dear God, I am Facing the truth of how I have been hurt by [offense]. I know you are intimately aware of my suffering and care about my pain. Please heal me; I am Releasing my wounds to you. I know that your judgements are perfect and that you desire for everyone to repent of sin and receive forgiveness through Christ, just as I received your gift of forgiveness. So, I choose to forgive [name]. I Entrust my claims for justice to you. I surrender my rights for apologies, paybacks, bitterness or using this sin to my advantage. These debts are now yours to repay. Your justice and vengeance for sin are perfect. I accept them as my own. Jesus, I praise and Exalt you for paying my debts and becoming a peace offering to the world- even to those who have hurt me! Help me love [name] with your love by the supernatural power of the Holy Spirit who lives in me. Amen.

Forgivenss Tips

God loves you.

Begin with your resentments and Harms to Me inventories.

Remember F.R.E.E.

Specifically name sins to God.

God does not need forgiveness.

You can't forgive yourself.

Wounds from a friend can be trusted.

Came back later if you are stuck, but do not ignore forgiving a sin.

Amends Truths

Making amends is not taking all responsibility for a conflict.

Making amends is not excusing someone else's sin.

Making amends is not denying your hurt or stuffing your anger.

Making amends should not be avoided just because someone is not aware of your sin.

Making amends does not free you from all consequences.

Making amends is not reconciliation.

Characteristics of Biblical Amends

A humble attitude

An honest and specific confession of sin

An apology

A request for forgiveness

Willingness to make restitution for losses

Readiness to share Christ

Steps of change

Amends Tips

Remember God is good.

Pray.

Your job is to be faithful.

Stick to your amends only, no reasons/causes/other person's amends.

Don't go through amends alone.

Don't settle for less than God's best.

Confess the full truth to those injured by your sin.

Be willing to accept the appropiate consequences.

Give others time to process the hurt.

Cut off all ties to the sin that trapped you previously and do as much as is apporopiate to make restituion for your sin.

Remain devoted to Christ and follow him.
